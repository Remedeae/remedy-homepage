Sorry who are you?

The tale began over 30 cycles ago, somewhere in the forests of Sweden where a tiny lass was born with a crayon in her hand. As most children I loved to draw and early showed a sense of artistery with the comment "The wallpaper needed blue flowers." after remediying the fact that there was only pink flowers on said wallpaper.
The other thing I aboslutely obsessed over as a kid was knights. Lord in heaven I wanted to be a knight when I grew up, but you know reality and the government doesn't really let you do that. Anyhow my dad promised me an armor when I stopped growing, but I ended up beeing more into motorcycles at that age so that didn't happen. But I still remained a nerd at heart so when Halloween came around it was always a blast. Then one day I stumbeled over this thing called 'Cosplay' and went "THE WHAT NOW? People make costumes and wear them at conventions all year round?"
9 years later and here we are.
